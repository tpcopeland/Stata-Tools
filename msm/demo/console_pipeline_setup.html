msm - Marginal Structural Models for Stata
Version 1.0.0
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Data Preparation
  ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  msm_prepare     Map variable names, validate data structure.
                Entry point for the pipeline. Stores
                metadata in dataset characteristics.

  msm_validate    Comprehensive data quality checks: person-
                period format, gaps, treatment variation,
                positivity, and missing data patterns.

Core Engine
  ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  msm_weight      Stabilized inverse probability of treatment
                weights (IPTW) with optional IPCW for
                informative censoring. Logistic models
                with cumulative product via log-sum.

  msm_fit         Weighted outcome model: pooled logistic
                regression (GLM), linear, or Cox PH.
                Robust/sandwich SE clustered by individual.

  msm_predict     Counterfactual predictions under always-
                treated vs never-treated strategies.
                Monte Carlo CIs via Cholesky decomposition.

Diagnostics & Reporting
  ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  msm_diagnose    Weight distribution, effective sample size,
                covariate balance (SMD before/after).

  msm_plot        Visualization: weight densities, Love plots,
                survival curves, treatment trajectories,
                and positivity assessment.

  msm_report      Publication-quality tables: analysis summary,
                weight diagnostics, model coefficients.
                Export to display, CSV, or Excel.

  msm_protocol    MSM study protocol: 7-component specification
                adapted from Robins, Hernan & Brumback.

  msm_sensitivity E-value (VanderWeele & Ding 2017) and
                confounding strength bounds for
                unmeasured confounding assessment.

  msm, status      Inspect the current pipeline stage, mapped
                variables, and saved artifacts.

msm_protocol - MSM Study Protocol
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  1. Population
     Adults followed for 10 discrete periods (N=500; 5000 person-period observations)

  2. Treatment strategies
     Dynamic treatment assignment at each period; counterfactual contrast is always treated versus never treated

  3. Confounders
     Time-varying: biomarker, comorbidity; baseline: age, sex; informative censoring indicator

  4. Outcome
     Binary outcome assessed each period and summarized as cumulative incidence over follow-up

  5. Causal contrast
     Risk difference in cumulative incidence under always-treated versus never-treated strategies

  6. Weight specification
     Stabilized IPTW with baseline numerator model, denominator model including time-varying covariates, and 1st/99th percentile truncation

  7. Statistical analysis
     Validated pooled logistic MSM with quadratic time trend, robust SEs, Monte Carlo prediction intervals, and E-value sensitivity analysis
